About AdNow

Building trust in online marketplace connections

Our Mission

AdNow was created to solve a common problem: finding trustworthy sellers online. We believe that buying and selling should be safe, simple, and direct.

Our platform connects verified sellers with buyers across Ghana, enabling direct communication through familiar channels like WhatsApp, Instagram, and Snapchat.

Why Choose AdNow?

We're different because we prioritize trust and quality

Verified Sellers

Every seller on AdNow goes through a verification process. We check business credentials, product quality, and customer feedback before approval.

Trust and Safety

Our vetting process helps protect you from scams. We only list sellers who have proven their legitimacy and commitment to customer satisfaction.

Direct Contact

No middlemen, no complex payment systems. Contact sellers directly through WhatsApp, Instagram, or Snapchat to discuss products and arrange purchases.

Easy Search

Our powerful search and filter system helps you find exactly what you're looking for. Browse by category, location, or keyword.

Featured Sellers

Our featured sellers represent the best of what AdNow has to offer. They've earned premium placement through excellent service and ratings.

Mobile Friendly

Access AdNow from any device. Our responsive design ensures a seamless experience whether you're on phone, tablet, or desktop.
