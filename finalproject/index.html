Site Name

AdNow

Why This Name?

  • "Ad" - Represents advertising and advertisements, the core function of the platform
  • "Now" - Emphasizes instant access and immediate connection to sellers
  • The name is short, memorable, and easy to spell
  • It clearly communicates the platform's purpose: advertising products right now
  • Domain-friendly and brandable

Potential Domain: adnow.com, adnow.gh, getadnow.com

Site Purpose

AdNow is a marketplace advertising platform designed to bridge the gap between trusted sellers and consumers. The platform serves two primary audiences:

For Consumers

  • Search and filter products easily
  • Find verified and vetted sellers
  • Access direct contact information (WhatsApp, Instagram, Snapchat)
  • Browse featured sellers for quick discovery
  • View seller profiles with product images, videos, and rate cards

For Sellers

  • Create professional seller profiles
  • Showcase products with images and videos
  • Connect directly with potential customers
  • Get featured placement for increased visibility
  • Build trust through platform verification

Core Value Proposition: AdNow provides a trusted environment where only vetted and approved sellers can advertise, ensuring consumers find reliable products and services quickly.

Scenarios

These scenarios represent questions that our target audience might ask when visiting AdNow:

Scenario 1: Consumer Looking for Products

"I want to buy affordable sneakers in Accra. How can I find trusted sellers who won't scam me, and how do I contact them directly?"

How AdNow Answers: The search and filter functionality allows users to find sneaker sellers by location. Each seller profile displays verification badges, product images, and direct WhatsApp/Instagram contact links.

Scenario 2: New Seller Wanting Exposure

"I sell handmade jewelry on Instagram but I'm not getting enough customers. How can I reach more buyers and get my products seen by more people?"

How AdNow Answers: The seller registration form allows new sellers to create profiles. The featured listing option provides premium placement on the homepage for maximum visibility.

Scenario 3: Consumer Checking Seller Credibility

"How do I know if a seller on this platform is legitimate and won't take my money without delivering?"

How AdNow Answers: The About page explains the vetting process. Verified sellers display trust badges, and users can view seller ratings and product galleries before making contact.

Scenario 4: Seller Wanting Premium Features

"How much does it cost to get my business featured on the homepage, and how do I apply for a featured listing?"

How AdNow Answers: The "For Sellers" page outlines featured listing packages with pricing, and includes a contact form to reach management for premium placement inquiries.

Typography

The font selections prioritize readability, modern aesthetics, and professional appearance.

Primary Font: Poppins

Poppins Bold (700)
Poppins SemiBold (600)
Poppins Regular (400)

Usage:

  • All headings (h1, h2, h3, h4, h5, h6)
  • Navigation links
  • Buttons
  • Logo text

Why Poppins? Geometric sans-serif with friendly, modern appearance. Excellent readability at all sizes and conveys professionalism.

Secondary Font: Open Sans

Open Sans Medium (500)
Open Sans Regular (400)

Usage:

  • Body paragraphs
  • Form labels and inputs
  • Card descriptions
  • Footer content

Why Open Sans? Highly legible humanist sans-serif optimized for screen reading. Pairs excellently with Poppins.

Typography Examples

Heading 1 - Poppins Bold 2.5rem

Heading 2 - Poppins SemiBold 2rem

Heading 3 - Poppins SemiBold 1.5rem

Body Text - Open Sans Regular 1rem. This is how regular paragraph text will appear throughout the website. The line height is set to 1.6 for optimal readability.

Small/Meta Text - Open Sans Regular 0.875rem
