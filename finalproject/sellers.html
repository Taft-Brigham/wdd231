Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about selling on AdNow

How long does the verification process take?

Our verification process typically takes 2-3 business days. We'll review your application and may contact you for additional information if needed.

What documents do I need to provide?

For basic verification, you'll need a valid ID, business registration (if applicable), and photos of your products. Premium sellers may need additional documentation.

Can I upgrade my package later?

Yes! You can upgrade your package at any time. Simply contact our support team and we'll help you transition to your preferred plan.

How do I receive payment from customers?

AdNow is a listing platform - all transactions happen directly between you and the customer. You handle your own payments through whatever method you prefer.

What if I want to cancel my subscription?

You can cancel your Featured or Premium subscription at any time. Your listing will revert to the Basic (free) plan at the end of your billing period.
