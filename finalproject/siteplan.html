Site Purpose

AdNow is a trusted marketplace advertising platform designed to bridge the gap between verified sellers and consumers. The platform creates a safe, efficient environment where users can discover products and connect directly with sellers.

üõí

For Consumers

AdNow helps buyers find exactly what they're looking for:

  • Search and filter products by category, location, or keyword
  • Browse only verified and vetted sellers for peace of mind
  • Access direct contact information (WhatsApp, Instagram, Snapchat)
  • Discover featured sellers on the homepage without searching
  • View detailed seller profiles with product images, videos, and rate cards
üíº

For Sellers

AdNow provides sellers with visibility and credibility:

  • Create professional seller profiles to showcase your business
  • Upload product images and videos to attract customers
  • Connect directly with potential buyers through social media
  • Get featured placement on the homepage for increased exposure
  • Build trust through platform verification and approval process

Core Value Proposition

AdNow runs on trust. Only vetted and approved sellers can advertise on the platform, ensuring that consumers find reliable products and services quickly. Featured sellers get premium placement, while all users benefit from a safe, scam-free marketplace environment.

Scenarios

Scenarios represent questions that our target audience might ask when visiting AdNow. These questions help drive the content and features of the website.

01

Consumer Looking for Products

"I want to buy affordable sneakers in my area. How can I find trusted sellers who won't scam me, and how do I contact them directly without going through middlemen?"

How AdNow Answers This

The homepage features a prominent search bar with category filters. Users can search for "sneakers" and filter by location. Each seller profile displays a verification badge, product gallery with images, and direct contact buttons for WhatsApp, Instagram, and Snapchat.

02

New Seller Wanting Exposure

"I sell handmade jewelry on Instagram but I'm not getting enough customers. How can I reach more buyers and get my products seen by people who are actively looking to purchase?"

How AdNow Answers This

The "For Sellers" page provides a registration form where new sellers can apply to join the platform. After verification, sellers can create profiles with product images and videos. Sellers can also apply for featured listing placement on the homepage for maximum visibility.

03

Consumer Checking Seller Credibility

"How do I know if a seller on this platform is legitimate and won't take my money without delivering the products I ordered?"

How AdNow Answers This

The "About" page explains AdNow's rigorous vetting process for all sellers. Verified sellers display a trust badge on their profiles. Users can also view seller ratings, read reviews, and browse product galleries with real images before making contact.

04

Seller Wanting Premium Features

"How much does it cost to get my business featured on the homepage, and what is the process to apply for a premium listing spot?"

How AdNow Answers This

The "For Sellers" page outlines all advertising packages including featured listing options and pricing tiers. A contact form allows sellers to reach management directly to inquire about premium placement. Payment information and listing duration details are clearly displayed.

Typography

The typography selections prioritize readability, modern aesthetics, and professional appearance. Two complementary fonts are used to create visual hierarchy.

Primary Font: Poppins

Poppins Bold (700) - ABCDEFGHIJKLMNOPQRSTUVWXYZ

Poppins SemiBold (600) - abcdefghijklmnopqrstuvwxyz

Poppins Regular (400) - 0123456789

Usage

  • All headings (H1, H2, H3, H4, H5, H6)
  • Navigation links
  • Button text
  • Logo and brand text
  • Featured labels and badges

Why Poppins?

Poppins is a geometric sans-serif with a friendly, modern appearance. It has excellent readability at all sizes and conveys professionalism while remaining approachable. Perfect for a marketplace that wants to build trust.

Secondary Font: Open Sans

Open Sans SemiBold (600) - ABCDEFGHIJKLMNOPQRSTUVWXYZ

Open Sans Medium (500) - abcdefghijklmnopqrstuvwxyz

Open Sans Regular (400) - 0123456789

Usage

  • Body paragraphs and descriptions
  • Form labels and input text
  • Card content and descriptions
  • Footer content
  • Meta information and small text

Why Open Sans?

Open Sans is a highly legible humanist sans-serif specifically optimized for screen reading. It pairs excellently with Poppins and ensures comfortable reading for longer text blocks like product descriptions.
